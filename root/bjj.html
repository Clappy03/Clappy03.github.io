Brazilain jiu jistu

Brazilain jiu jistu is martial art thats based around ground fighting and submission holds. it focuses on getting your opponent to the ground, controlling ones opponent and gain a dominant position to force them to "tap out"

the reason i chose to start jiu jistu is because im a big fan of the ufc and wanted to learn some of the fundimentals of fighting from the ground that i see all my favourite fighters do.

what i have gotten out of it

starting jiu jistu has made me alot more active and has made me more confident as a person. i have met many new people while going to sessions and has helped me how to navigate nottigham as they are based on the city campus
